Two-Tier Nutrition System

Professional meal planning that mirrors how real nutritionists work

Diet Plan (Strategic)

Your 30-day nutritional framework defining daily calorie targets and macro distribution. Ensures consistency and metabolic stability.

Daily Menu (Tactical)

Specific meal suggestions generated daily based on your diet plan and meal history. Provides variety while maintaining nutritional goals.

Why This Approach?

Consistency: All daily menus meet your long-term health goals
Cost Efficient: Diet plan generated once, used for 30 days
Professional: Mirrors nutritionist methodology
Adaptive: Plans regenerate every 30 days as you progress
